## FAQ

**Q: How long until PRISM ranks #1?**
A: 90-180 days if executed consistently. Week 4 checkpoint will show if strategy is working. Primary keywords should appear in top 10 by week 4, top 3 by week 8.

**Q: Should I do paid ads?**
A: Not in first 90 days. Organic strategy is sufficient for startup phase. Paid ads can amplify after organic foundation is strong.

**Q: What about GitHub ranking?**
A: GitHub has its own ranking algorithm. Focus on Awesome Lists (they drive GitHub visibility) + product quality + engagement.

**Q: Do I need to match "PRISM" exactly?**
A: No. Search for "Python MRO tool" or "method resolution order" or "which method runs Python" — PRISM will rank because you're solving the exact problem.

**Q: What about misspellings like "prisma" or "prism-tools.io"?**
A: Schema markup + synonyms in JSON-LD capture variants. Consider owning prism-tools.com domain + 301 redirect.

**Q: How do I measure success?**
A: Use the weekly milestone spreadsheet. Track: organic traffic (GA4), keyword rankings (GSC), backlinks (Ahrefs), social followers (Twitter).
